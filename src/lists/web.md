I made this small corner of _The Web_
using text files full of
instructions for computers
(aka _code_).

<!-- intro -->

The web is an experimental art project
designed to give people more agency
in an interactive ecosystem.
You can customize the fonts you use
to read my website on screens of any size and shape!
I enjoy pushing those features even farther,
but they are _fundamental to the design of the platform_.

It's a platform for finding community and sharing our humanity.
And it's being overrun by extractive strip-mining corporations.
Cheers!
