I enjoy art that bleeds across genres and forms.
I want an artistic practice
that is _curious and expansive_.

<!-- intro -->

[Grapefruit Lab](https://grapefruitlab.com/)
and [Teacup Gorilla](https://teacupgorilla.com/)
are constantly collaborating on
multi-sensory performances
in Denver.
But I also have my hands in
clock repair, yarn,
and (especially) [pottery](/pottery/).
