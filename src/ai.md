There's nothing inherently wrong with
automation or even 'machine learning'
except for the misleading name.
But I have no interest in language-guessing machines
built on surveillance and exploited labor --
sold back to us as a replacement for expertise.

<!-- intro -->

I find the entire project offensive,
insulting, and degrading.
I have no interest in trusting
a black-box information-shuffling device
hawked by weapons contractors
and the worst guys on Sir Tim's World Wide Web.

It's a product designed to launder bias
as mathematical truth,
and deflect responsibility
onto a fancy slab of electrified rocks.
People will tell you that _it works_,
and I agree.
But we disagree about what it's designed to do.

**Everything on this site is put here by me,
a human,
because it's something I want to share.**
