---
title: There's no point resisting
date: 2026-06-16T16:05:21-06:00
tags: [ai]
---

I'm afraid nuclear weapons are here to stay.
There's no point resisting.

I'm afraid assault weapons
and school shootings in the US are here to stay.
I'm afraid cars are here to stay.
Corporate social media,
I imagine,
is also here to stay?
Addiction-honed algorithms,
and constantly expanding surveillance --
on and offline?

<!-- intro -->

To quote one Marx or another:
_I'm against it_.

We too easily conflate
"_this is not going away_"
(whatever that means)
with "_there's no point resisting_"

It seems to me like the reverse is true.
The longer something harmful has-been or will-be around,
the more resistance is essential
in limiting that harm.

In fact,
if _you_ are interested
in keeping something around
despite the well-documented harms --
maybe _you_ ought to be right out front
in doing that resistance work.

My job as a designer/developer
is not to hype new tech,
but to carefully protect my communities
against it's mis-use.
